Welcome to IT Help Desk

  IT Help Desk is a forum where you can ask for help with a variety of computer and technology related topics. Topics are orginized by category for easy navigation and so you can get the best help as fast as possible. To view all the topics, begin by visiting the forum index. There you can view all threads related to a topic, reply to an exiting thread, or create your own thread. In addition to help topics, we also have categories for general discussion about several topics relating to technology or off-topic discussion. Feel free to contact us about any categories you feel should be added to the forum.


  Site guests can view any topic but must register for an account before making or replying to any threads. Visit the User Profile page to view or edit account information.

Intel Launches Rocket Lake 11th Gen Core i9, Core i7, and Core i5
by Dr. Ian Cutress

A new Intel 11th Generation Core desktop prosser has launched called Rocket Lake. This generation sees the return of the Core i9, i7, and i5 models. The i7 and i5 models feature 8 cores / 16 threads while the i5 models feature 6 cores/ 12 threads. These processors have base frequencies up to 3.9GHz and turbo speeds beyond 5GHz. This generation also boasts support for DDR4-3200, overclocking functionality, Intel UHD Graphics, and up to 20 PICe 4.0 lanes. Click the image to the left to visit the article.
